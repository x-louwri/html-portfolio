Top 3 Movies of All time

This movie is an emotional masterpiece, I love the spin on the God's creation of mankind through Genesis and Adam. Beautiful story telling.

Ghost in a Shell

I love this movie because of how it displays the potentail future of cybernetics and AI.

Alita Battle Angel

Very heart-pulling story with the perfect amount of sci-fi.
